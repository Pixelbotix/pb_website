Who We Are

PixelBotix Technologies Pvt. Ltd. is a Future-driven product innovation and EdTech company pioneering the convergence of Robotics, Artificial Intelligence, Smart Manufacturing, and Emerging Technologies. Founded by a passionate team of researchers, inventors, and educators, we are on a mission to turn cutting-edge research into accessible, real-world solutions and empower minds through immersive, hands-on learning.

At the heart of PixelBotix is a leadership team with deep expertise across Robotics, Computer vision, AI and ML, Healthcare Technologies, Additive manufacturing, Smart manufacturing, UAV, AMR, Intelligent assistive devices, and STEM experts. With multiple patents, ground-breaking innovations, and years of mentoring experience, our founders blend academic excellence with practical impact.

PixelBotix Brainy babies, Little techies, Maker school, Future innovators, are our Edverse platform, PixelBotix Skill Labs is our hands-on learning platform, and PixelBotix AI & Robotics Hub is a state-of-the-art research and development platform hosting a series of specialized labs in cutting-edge domains.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower learners, innovators, and industries through cutting-edge research, accessible education, and practical solutions in Robotics, AI, and Emerging Technologies—bridging the gap between imagination and real-world impact.

Our Vision

To become a global leader in deep-tech innovation and education by transforming the way people learn, build, and interact with intelligent systems shaping a smarter, inclusive, and future-ready world.
