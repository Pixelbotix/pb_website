About PixelBotix

PixelBotix Technologies Pvt. Ltd. is a future-driven product innovation and EdTech company pioneering the convergence of Robotics, Artificial Intelligence, Smart Manufacturing, and Emerging Technologies. Founded by a passionate team of researchers, inventors, and educators, we are on a mission to transform cutting-edge research into accessible, real-world solutions while empowering minds through immersive, hands-on learning. At the heart of PixelBotix is a leadership team with deep expertise across Robotics, Computer Vision, AI and ML, Healthcare Technologies, Additive Manufacturing, Smart Manufacturing, UAV, AMR, Intelligent Assistive Devices, and STEM education. With multiple patents, groundbreaking innovations, and years of mentoring experience, our founders blend academic excellence with practical impact.

Innovation in Motion

PixelBotix Brainy Babies, Little Techies, Maker School, and Future Innovators form our Edverse ecosystem, while PixelBotix Skill Labs serves as our hands-on learning platform. The PixelBotix AI & Robotics Hub stands as our state-of-the-art research and development center, hosting a series of specialized labs dedicated to exploring the frontiers of robotics, AI, and emerging technologies.

Changing Your Idea of What We Can Do

Driving innovation and inspiration through education, technology, and human imagination.

Our Mission

To empower learners, innovators, and industries through cutting-edge research, accessible education, and practical solutions in Robotics, AI, and Emerging Technologies — bridging the gap between imagination and real-world impact.

Our Vision

To become a global leader in deep-tech innovation and education by transforming the way people learn, build, and interact with intelligent systems — shaping a smarter, inclusive, and future-ready world.

Dr. Sabique P V

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Dr. Sabique P.V. is a trailblazer in the fields of Robotics, AI&ML, Computer Vision, and Robotic-assisted surgery, with over a decade of experience in cutting-edge research, innovation, and product development. As the Founder and CEO of PixelBotix, he has transformed his passion for robotics into a mission to make innovative products and STEM and AI education accessible for learners of all ages.

Holding a Ph.D. in Robotics from MIT, Dr. Sabique has spearheaded numerous high-impact R&D projects, published several influential journal papers, and is a prolific inventor with five patents. His innovations include the Automatic Coconut Harvester, Intelligent Assistive devices, an automated system for monitoring biological activities of mice, and vision-based pathogen detection devices, which have been successfully transferred to industry.

Under his leadership, PixelBotix is not just a company, it is a movement to empower the next generation of thinkers, creators, and problem-solvers in the realm of intelligent technologies.

Mr . Elangovan Anand

Co-Founder & Chief Technology Officer

Anand Elangovan is a multidisciplinary engineer and data enthusiast with a strong background in machine learning, computer vision, and algorithmic systems. He has worked on cutting-edge AI projects, building intelligent solutions that optimize processes and enhance automation across industries.

At PixelBotix, Anand drives the R&D of next-generation robotics technologies, focusing on innovation, prototyping, and intelligent system design. He leads the development of robotics products that combine AI, automation, and engineering excellence to solve real-world challenges.

With a blend of academic knowledge, industry experience, and a passion for hands-on innovation, Anand is committed to advancing PixelBotix’s mission to empower the next generation of creators, engineers, and thinkers through cutting-edge robotics and research.

Dr. Sivaperumal R

Co-Founder & Chief Development Officer

Dr. Sivaperumal R is a passionate innovator and technologist specializing in Additive Manufacturing, Robotics, and Advanced Materials. With a Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering from Anna University – MIT Campus, he brings a decade of research, product development, and educational experience to the forefront of technological transformation.

Beyond engineering, Dr. Sivaperumal is a mentor, educator, and speaker who has inspired students and professionals through hands-on workshops and industry collaborations. His vision is to make emerging technologies accessible, affordable, and impactful for the next generation of innovators.

As Co-Founder and CDO of PixelBotix, he leads R&D initiatives and the design of intelligent, real-world solutions from crashworthy automotive structures to biomedical devices and heritage preservation using 3D printing. He is also the founder of Mithra 3D Tech, a leading 3D printing and prototyping venture.
