About the Workshop

This intensive offline, hands-on workshop focuses on the revolutionary integration of AI technologies with 3D printing processes. Designed to address the growing demand for smart manufacturing solutions, participants will explore how artificial intelligence enhances additive manufacturing workflows, from design optimization to quality control. This workshop is essential for engineering students who want to stay ahead in the rapidly evolving landscape of Industry 4.0 and smart manufacturing technologies.
