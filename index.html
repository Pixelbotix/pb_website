Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our programs, technologies, and services.

Our educational programs are designed for children and young adults from ages 0 to 25, with age-appropriate curricula:

  • PixelBotix Brainy Babies: Ages 0-5
  • PixelBotix Little Techies: Ages 5-9
  • PixelBotix Maker School: Ages 9-17
  • PixelBotix Future Innovators: Ages 16-25

Each program is tailored to the developmental stage and learning capabilities of the respective age group.

Schools can partner with us through our PixelBotix Skill Labs program. The process typically involves:

  1. Initial consultation to understand your school's needs and objectives
  2. Customized proposal with equipment, curriculum, and budget options
  3. Implementation plan including installation and setup
  4. Teacher training and ongoing support
  5. Curriculum integration and assessment frameworks

Contact our educational partnerships team to schedule an initial consultation.

Students work with a wide range of technologies based on their age group and program level:

  • Programming languages: Block-based coding for younger students; Python, C++, JavaScript for advanced students
  • Hardware: Educational robots, microcontrollers (Arduino, Raspberry Pi), sensors, actuators
  • Software tools: CAD software, simulation tools, AI/ML frameworks
  • Fabrication: 3D printing, laser cutting, basic electronics
  • Advanced topics (for older students): AI, computer vision, IoT, robotics, drone technology

All technologies are introduced progressively, with age-appropriate complexity and safety considerations.

  • Live online classes with interactive components
  • Hybrid programs combining online learning with periodic in-person workshops
  • Self-paced courses with mentor support

While we believe in the value of hands-on, in-person learning for technical skills, our remote options are designed to be as interactive and engaging as possible.

Businesses can collaborate with our R&D team in several ways:

  • Sponsored research projects in our specialized labs
  • Joint development of products or technologies
  • Consulting services for technical challenges
  • Prototype development and testing
  • Technology transfer and licensing
  • Custom training programs for your technical teams

Our business development team can discuss your specific needs and create a customized collaboration framework. We have experience working with startups, SMEs, and large corporations across various industries.

Students receive different types of certifications depending on the program:

  • Program completion certificates for all age groups
  • Skill-specific certifications for advanced topics (AI, robotics, IoT, etc.)
  • Project portfolio documentation
  • Competition and achievement certificates

For older students (Future Innovators program), we also offer industry-recognized certifications through our partnerships with technology companies and educational institutions. These certifications can be valuable for college applications and early career opportunities.
