Advanced Technologies

FDM Technology

  • • Cost-effective production
  • • Large build volumes
  • • Multiple material options

SLA Technology

Stereolithography for ultra-high resolution prints with smooth surface finishes and fine details.

  • • Exceptional detail quality
  • • Smooth surface finish
  • • Biocompatible resins

SLS Technology

Selective Laser Sintering for complex geometries and functional parts without support structures.

  • • No support structures needed
  • • Complex internal geometries
  • • Production-grade materials

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our 3D printing services and processes.

We accept all standard 3D file formats including STL, OBJ, PLY, 3MF, and STEP files. Our team can also work with CAD files from SolidWorks, AutoCAD, Fusion 360, and other popular design software. If you have a different format, please contact us and we'll help you convert it.

Most standard projects are completed within 24-48 hours. Complex or large-scale projects may take 3-7 days depending on the size, material, and finishing requirements. Rush orders can often be accommodated for an additional fee. We'll provide you with an accurate timeline when you submit your project.

Yes, we provide comprehensive post-processing services including sanding, polishing, painting, vapor smoothing, and assembly. We can also apply various surface treatments such as electroplating, anodizing, and custom color matching to meet your specific requirements.

Our print sizes range from miniature parts as small as 1mm to large components up to 400mm x 400mm x 500mm depending on the technology used. For larger projects, we can print in sections and provide assembly services. There's no minimum order quantity - we handle everything from single prototypes to production runs.

We maintain strict quality control processes including pre-print file analysis, calibrated equipment, controlled environment conditions, and post-print inspection using precision measuring tools. All our technicians are certified and we follow ISO 9001 quality management standards to ensure consistent, high-quality results.
