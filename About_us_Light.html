Who We Are

Founded in 2018 by a team of passionate engineers and AI specialists, our company has been at the forefront of robotics innovation for over 7 years. We believe that robotics technology has the power to solve some of humanity's most pressing challenges.

Our commitment to robotics education stems from our conviction that the next generation of innovators needs hands-on experience with cutting-edge technology. Through our educational programs, we've empowered over 15,000 students across 28 countries to pursue careers in STEM fields.

At the core of our philosophy is the belief that technology should enhance human potential, not replace it. Our robots are designed to work alongside humans, augmenting their capabilities and freeing them to focus on what they do best: creative problem-solving and innovation.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To democratize access to robotics technology and education, enabling individuals and organizations of all sizes to harness the power of automation and artificial intelligence to solve real-world problems.

Our Vision

A world where intelligent robots work harmoniously alongside humans, enhancing our capabilities, improving our quality of life, and helping us address the most significant challenges facing our planet and society.
